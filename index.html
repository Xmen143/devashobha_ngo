Our Impact Areas

Transforming Lives Through Action

We work across six key areas to create sustainable change and empower communities across India. Each initiative is designed to address critical social challenges and create lasting positive impact.

Women Empowerment

Empowering women through skill development, financial literacy, and leadership training. We provide microfinance opportunities, vocational training, and create platforms for women to become financially independent and community leaders.

  • Self-Help Group Formation
  • Microfinance & Banking Linkage
  • Vocational Training Programs
  • Leadership Development
  • Digital Literacy Training
Learn More

Skill Development

Creating employment opportunities through comprehensive skill development programs. We offer training in various trades, digital skills, and entrepreneurship to help individuals build sustainable livelihoods.

  • Technical Skills Training
  • Digital & IT Literacy
  • Entrepreneurship Development
  • Placement Assistance
  • Certification Programs
Learn More

Elder Care

Providing compassionate care and support to elderly citizens through specialized care facilities, health monitoring, and social engagement programs. We ensure dignity and quality of life for our senior community members.

  • Residential Care Facilities
  • Health Monitoring Services
  • Social Engagement Programs
  • Nutritional Support
  • Emergency Response System
Learn More

Education

Transforming lives through quality education initiatives. We run schools, provide scholarships, and create learning opportunities for children from underprivileged backgrounds to break the cycle of poverty.

  • Primary & Secondary Schools
  • Scholarship Programs
  • Digital Learning Platforms
  • Teacher Training
  • Infrastructure Development
Learn More

Healthcare

Ensuring accessible healthcare for all through mobile clinics, health camps, and awareness programs. We focus on preventive care, maternal health, and treatment of common diseases in rural and urban areas.

  • Mobile Medical Units
  • Health Camps & Screenings
  • Maternal & Child Health
  • Vaccination Programs
  • Health Awareness Campaigns
Learn More

Environment

Protecting our planet through environmental conservation initiatives. We work on afforestation, waste management, renewable energy promotion, and climate change awareness to create a sustainable future.

  • Tree Plantation Drives
  • Waste Management Programs
  • Renewable Energy Projects
  • Water Conservation
  • Climate Awareness Campaigns
Learn More

Creating Lasting Change Since 2008

Devashobha Foundation has been at the forefront of social transformation in India, working tirelessly to address the most pressing challenges facing our communities. Our holistic approach ensures sustainable development and long-term impact.

Founded with the vision of creating an equitable society, we have evolved into one of India's most trusted NGOs, implementing evidence-based programs that create measurable change in the lives of millions.

  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified Organization
  • 12A & 80G Registered with Income Tax
  • FCRA Approved for International Funding
  • Guidestar India Transparency Certified
  • NITI Aayog Registered NGO
