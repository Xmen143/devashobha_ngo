Who We Are

Devashobha Sanstha Trust is a non-profit organization based in Mumbai, dedicated to the care, dignity, and holistic well-being of elderly citizens who are destitute, neglected, or abandoned. We offer long-term residential care, medical services, and emotional support in a nurturing environment.

Why Choose Us?

  • Peaceful and hygienic Mumbai-based residence
  • Round-the-clock caregiving with medical tie-ups
  • Home-style nutritious meals
  • Festive celebrations and cultural integration
  • Legal support and senior citizen rights advocacy

Our Old Age Home Project

This initiative establishes a fully functional 30-bed old age home in Mumbai for abandoned and needy senior citizens. We ensure:

  • Safe, supervised accommodation
  • 3 meals/day + evening tea & snacks
  • Doctor visits, medicine support, emergency services
  • Recreation, group activities, digital literacy & counseling

CSR Requirement: ₹3.48 Cr for 3 years
