About Us

Devashobha Sanstha Trust is a registered NGO dedicated to the care and rehabilitation of abandoned and needy senior citizens in Mumbai. We are committed to providing holistic shelter, medical support, and emotional well-being to those who have no one left to care for them.

Our Old Age Home Project

We are establishing a 30-bed residential care facility in Mumbai to support destitute senior citizens. Services will include:

  • 24/7 Shelter and Nutrition
  • Medical Check-ups and Emergency Care
  • Yoga, Therapy, and Counseling
  • Community Reintegration and Legal Aid
