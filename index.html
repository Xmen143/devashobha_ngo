Who We Are

Devashobha Sanstha Trust is a non-profit organization registered in Maharashtra, focused on supporting destitute and neglected senior citizens through compassionate care, community housing, and integrated social services.

Our Old Age Home Project

Located in Mumbai, our 30-bed residential facility provides comprehensive care for the elderly including:

  • Secure and hygienic shelter with nutritious meals
  • Medical services, emergency care, and rehabilitation
  • Spiritual wellness, group therapy, and recreation
  • Legal aid, family counseling, and social reintegration
